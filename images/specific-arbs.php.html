Ingredients

  • 2 Unicorn Berries
  • 1 Pixie Dust Sprinkles
  • 1/2 tbsp Dragon Scale Slices
  • 3 Mermaid Song Syrup
  • 9 Elf-made Whipped Cream
  • 2 Gnome-Grown Rainbow Grapes
  • 1/4 cup Fairy Tale Apples
  • 1/8 drop Enchanted Starfruit Chunks
  • 7 Magic Marshmallow Clouds
  • 1 Leprechaun Gold Leaf Garnish

Procedures

  • Wash and prepare the mixed berries. Place them in a bowl.
  • Drizzle the honey over the mixed berries. Gently stir to coat the berries in honey.
  • Spoon the whipped cream over the honey-coated berries.
  • Add the grapes, diced apple, and banana slices to the bowl.
  • If you want to make it extra fun, sprinkle mini marshmallows over the top for a sweet surprise.
  • Gently toss everything together until the fruit is well combined with the whipped cream and honey.
  • Serve your simple fantasy fruit salad immediately and enjoy your magical treat!
